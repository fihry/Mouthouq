
# Business Model Canvas

## Key Partners
- **Local Professionals**: Individuals and companies providing household services.
- **Training Organizations**: Potential partnerships to offer skill development for professionals.
- **Marketing Partners**: Collaborations with influencers, ad agencies, and content creators.

## Key Activities
- **Service Listings**: Enable professionals to register and manage their service offerings.
- **User Management**: Handle user registration, login, and account management.
- **Ratings & Reviews**: Implement a system for users to leave feedback on services and professionals.
- **Marketing**: Drive user acquisition through ads, social media, and word-of-mouth.

## Value Propositions
- **Convenience**: Users can find trusted professionals for various services in their area with ease.
- **Quality Assurance**: The review and rating system ensures high-quality service standards.
- **Affordable Pricing**: Competitive pricing through a subscription or commission model.
- **Real-Time Communication**: Chat and notifications provide an instant connection between users and professionals.

## Customer Relationships
- **Personalized Recommendations**: Based on user preferences and past interactions.
- **Loyalty Programs**: Rewarding users for engagement and referrals.
- **User Support**: Providing assistance through chat, email, or customer service.
